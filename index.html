Tik-Tak-Toe Game

How to play:

TikTakToe is a very simple game which can be played between two players, each player have to pick between "X" and "O" and click on the boxes to play a turn, when a player has occupied all 3 spaces horizontally,vertically or diagonally, that player wins!!!.
PS: Player 1 picks a letter and starts the first round but after that, Anyone who wins a round plays first the next round.
